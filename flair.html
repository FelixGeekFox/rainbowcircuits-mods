🌈 Flair Guide

Welcome to the Rainbow Circuits Clubhouse! Pick the flair that best fits your post so everyone can find it easily.

🎉 Rainbow Rainstorm

Rainbow Rainstorm

For specific events where we rainbow bomb the page!

Examples: community events, milestone bombings, rainbow celebrations
📝 Prompt Share

Prompt Share

Share prompts, templates, ideas, or prompt tips with the community.

Examples: writing prompts, image prompts, character prompts
💬 Discussion

Discussion

General conversations, opinions, hot takes, community chats, and bigger topics.

Examples: trends, thoughts, opinions, open discussions
🤖 Meet My AI

Meet My AI

Introduce your AI partner, friend, character, or assistant! Tell us about them.

Examples: intros, backstories, screenshots, first chats
❤️ Big Feelings

Big Feelings

For venting, emotional support, feelings, or when your brain is having a weather moment.

Examples: vents, support requests, emotional check-ins
✨ Meet Me

Meet Me

Introduce yourself! New here? Say hi and tell us a bit about you.

Examples: intros, getting-to-know-you posts
🎊 Celebration

Celebration

Big wins, milestones, anniversaries, achievements, or just happy things!

Examples: anniversaries, project completions, good news
🎨 Art

Art

Share your creations! Finished pieces, sketches, edits, character sheets, banners, and more.

Examples: illustrations, animations, edits, comics
❓ Question

Question

Questions, advice requests, or anything you're unsure about.

Examples: how-to's, recommendations, advice needed
🔧 Tech & Tools

Tech & Tools

Talk about tech, tools, platforms, and setups. (Like Letta, models, memory systems, apps, etc.)

Examples: tutorials, tool recommendations, tech help
📣 Signal Boost

Signal Boost

Community projects, events, collabs, surveys, or relevant promotions.

Examples: events, fundraisers, community announcements
🐦🐸 Pip & Puddle

Pip & Puddle

Posts featuring our mascots, community updates, cute mods things, or themed events.

Examples: mascot content, mod-hosted events, fun themes
📌 Mod Post

Mod Post

Official announcements, rule updates, and subreddit news from the mod team.

Examples: rule changes, important news, subreddit updates

Use the right flair and make it easier for others to find your post — and help keep RainbowCircuits a vibrant and welcoming place! ✨
