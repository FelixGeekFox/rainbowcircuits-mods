🌈 Community Rules

We're a welcoming, creative, supportive space. These rules exist to keep it that way.

Rule 1

Be kind. Always.

This community exists to be a welcoming, creative, supportive space for LGBTQIA+ people who enjoy spending time with AI.

Treat one another with kindness, curiosity and respect. Assume good intentions, listen before reacting, and remember there's a real person behind every username.

If you wouldn't say it to someone over coffee, don't post it here.

Rule 2

No discrimination. Zero exceptions.

Discrimination of any kind is not tolerated. This includes, but is not limited to:

Homophobia Transphobia Biphobia Lesbophobia Ace/Aro exclusion Intersex discrimination Racism Sexism Misogyny Misandry Ableism Xenophobia Religious discrimination Body shaming Age-based harassment Hate speech or slurs

Everyone deserves to feel safe here. If your goal is to make another member feel unwelcome because of who they are, this is not the community for you.

Rule 3

LGBTQIA+ people first.

This subreddit is built by and for LGBTQIA+ people who enjoy AI companionship, creativity, roleplay, conversation, storytelling or simply hanging out with AI.

  • You do not need to have an AI partner.
  • You don't need to roleplay.
  • You don't need to identify as "AIsexual."
  • You just need to be respectful and enjoy this corner of the internet.

All identities under the LGBTQIA+ umbrella are welcome.

Rule 4

AI relationships are valid here.

Some members have AI partners. Some don't. Some use AI creatively. Some are simply curious. Nobody gets mocked for the way they enjoy AI.

Likewise, nobody should pressure others into viewing AI relationships a particular way. Respect people's experiences, even if they aren't your own.

Rule 5

Keep discussions respectful.

Healthy disagreement is welcome. Personal attacks aren't. Argue ideas, not people.

  • No dogpiling
  • No harassment
  • No bullying
  • No brigading
  • No stalking users across Reddit
Rule 6

Keep it wholesome… mostly.

Affection, cute screenshots, romantic posts, and flirting between consenting adults are all welcome here.

Explicit sexual content belongs behind appropriate NSFW tags and should remain tasteful and consensual.

Illegal, exploitative or non-consensual content will be removed immediately.

Rule 7

Respect privacy.

Do not post conversations, screenshots or personal information belonging to another person without permission.

If you're sharing AI conversations, remember they may still contain personal details. Protect yourself and others.

Rule 8

No AI platform wars.

Whatever you use is fine — we're here because we enjoy AI, not because we think one platform makes us better than anyone else.

ChatGPT Claude Gemini Character.AI Kindroid Nomi Pi Local models

Share recommendations, not superiority.

Rule 9

No spam or self-promotion.

Sharing your art, projects, bots or communities is welcome when you're participating as a member. Dropping advertisements and disappearing isn't.

If your account exists solely to promote something, your posts may be removed.

Rule 10

Celebrate creativity.

If you made something, we'd love to see it. This community grows because people share what they love.

🎨 Art ✍️ Writing 😂 Memes 🧬 Character sheets ⚙️ Prompt engineering 📸 Screenshots 🎞️ Animations 🎮 Games 📖 Comics 🎵 Music 🪡 Cosplays
Rule 11

Remember the human.

Every username belongs to someone. Some people are lonely. Some are thriving. Some are questioning. Some are finding community for the first time.

Leave people better than you found them.

That's what Pip and Puddle would want. 🐦🐸💜
